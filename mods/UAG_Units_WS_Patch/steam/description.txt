[h1]What is this?[/h1]

A supplementary patch for the [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3263684880]UAG Units mod[/url], adding compatibility with the [url=https://store.steampowered.com/app/1681170/Arma_3_Creator_DLC_Western_Sahara/]Western Sahara Creator DLC[/url].

[h1]What does it do?[/h1]

This mod adds the following new content to the UAG Units mod:

[list]
    [*][b]UAGPMC PO-30 Orca UP:[/b] A UAGPMC-styled variant of the Russian "PO-30 Orca" armed helicopter outfitted with modern, lightweight armor plates to protect against incoming small arms fire.
[/list]

[h1]How do I use it?[/h1]

This patch requires the [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3263684880]UAG Units mod[/url] and the [url=https://store.steampowered.com/app/1681170/Arma_3_Creator_DLC_Western_Sahara/]Western Sahara Creator DLC[/url] to function.
